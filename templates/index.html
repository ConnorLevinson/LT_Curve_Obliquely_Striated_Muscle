This website offers a way to explore the length-tension relationships found in obliquely striated muscles. These muscles differ from muscles we're familiar with because they have a distinct protein structure that influences how they produce tension. The application allows for users to choose values for the filament lengths, bare-zone lengths, and angle of striation. This was built using the research of Dr. Taylor-Burt and colleagues for educational and reasearch purposes.
